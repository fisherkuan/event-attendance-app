How to use this web app

Viewing Events

Events are displayed in a list on the main page. You can filter the events by time range (Future, Past, or All) and by calendar.

RSVPing for Events

To RSVP for an event, click the "+" button on the event card. To remove your RSVP, click the "-" button.

Adding Events

To add an event, you must be a member of the Google Group. Click the "Join group to add events" button to join the group. Once you are a member, you can add events to the calendar, and they will appear in the web app.
