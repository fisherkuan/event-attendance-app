Frequently asked

Questions, answered.

Everything you might wonder about RSVPs, calendars, donations, and how this little app is run.

How do RSVPs work?
Click the + button on an event card and enter your name. That's it — you're on the list. The attendance count updates in real time for everyone.
Can I cancel my RSVP?
Yes. Click the − button on the event card, pick your name from the list, and confirm. If you can't find your name, it may have been entered with a different spelling.
Where do my donations go?
Hosting fees — Heroku, Postgres, domain, and occasional Stripe fees. Every expense is logged on the donations page so you can see where money goes.
Can I organize my own event?
Yes! Join the community group (link in the top-right of the calendar), and you can add events directly to the shared Google Calendar. They'll appear here automatically.
Is my data shared with anyone?
No. Names entered for RSVPs are stored only to track attendance. Payments are handled by Stripe — the app never sees your card data.
